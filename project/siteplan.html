PhOne Solution

Site Name

This name represents a modern and reliable brand focused exclusively on selling sealed, brand-new smartphones. It highlights the idea of providing one trusted solution for customers looking for quality devices, warranty protection, and competitive prices—all supported by a professional online shopping experience.

Site Purpose

The site serves as a trusted online platform for purchasing sealed, brand-new smartphones. It provides detailed product information, warranty assurance, and competitive pricing. Customers can browse, compare, and confidently buy authentic devices through a professional and secure website experience.

Scenarios

  • How can I be sure that the smartphone I’m buying is brand new and not refurbished?
  • What warranty and after-sales support does PhOne Solution provide for its devices?
  • Are the prices competitive compared to other stores and sellers?
  • How do I navigate the website to find the right smartphone for my needs?

Color Schema

  • White (#FFFFFF)
  • Gray (#4A4A4A)
  • Blue (#007BFF)

Typography

  • Headings: Playfair Display — elegant serif font to add style and professionalism to titles
  • Body Text: Roboto — clean, sans-serif font for easy reading of descriptions and information

Wireframes

Mobile View: Vertical layout with the PhOne Solution logo and name at the top, a brief introduction, a product section with featured smartphones in a scrollable layout, and a fixed "Buy Now" or "Contact Us" button at the bottom for easy access.

Desktop View: Horizontal layout with the PhOne Solution logo and navigation menu at the top, a hero section introducing the brand, a featured smartphones section displayed in a grid format, followed by warranty information, pricing highlights, and a footer with contact details and social media links.
